CITY DESIGN

Vrikshara symbolizes circular design, flow, and harmony of life, reflecting the seamless connection between nature, people, and infrastructure.

City Motto (PE-TC)

Inspired by the harmonious integration of Policy & Energy (PE), the city’s vision is eternal growth, balance, and continuity.

City Planning & Zoning Overview

The plan divides the city into major zones, covering approximately **1500 km²**.

  • Central Zone: Includes Towers & Offices near transport nodes.
  • Lakes:Includes Vriksha Lake, used as a meeting point and tourist attraction.
  • Transport Corridor: Arterial roads seamlessly connect the city with surrounding towns.
  • Special Places:Includes the Eternal Pleasure Garden, Vriksha Palace, and National Parks.
